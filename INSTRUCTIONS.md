### Building Your Settlement

Tap structures in the **Settlement** tab to build them. Each has:
- A **cost** (what you spend)
- An **effect** (what you gain)
- A **cap** (how many you can build)

Start with **Shelters** to house more people and **Farms** to feed them. As your settlement grows, new structures unlock.

### Tips for New Players

1. **Balance growth and stores.** Population eats food. Don't expand faster than you can feed.

2. **Build farms early.** Food is your foundation.

3. **Explore when stable.** Expeditions cost resources you might need for emergencies.

4. **Read the events.** The writing holds clues about the valley's mysteries.

5. **Time moves on its own.** The game runs even while you're away (up to 24 hours of progress).

6. **Save often.** Use the save button in the header, or let auto-save do its work.
